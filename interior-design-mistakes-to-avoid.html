5 Common Interior Design Mistakes to Avoid

Don't let these simple errors ruin your home's aesthetic potential. 1. Pushing all furniture against the walls makes a room feel static. 2. Hanging art too high disconnects it from the furniture. 3. Poor rug sizing can make a large room look disconnected.

4. Matching everything too perfectly often results in a space losing its character. 5. Ignoring traffic patterns makes a beautiful room impractical. Avoiding these simple mistakes can instantly elevate your home to a professional level.
